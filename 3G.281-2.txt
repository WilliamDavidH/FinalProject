"I, Thomas Ross of the state aforesaid, Mariner…for and in consideration of the sum of one hundred Guineas to me in hand well and truly paid…and for divers other good causes and considerations me thereunto especially moving, 	have manumitted, enfranchised, set free and discharged…my Negro Slave named Ompey of and from all and all manner of Bondage and Slavery whatsoever…"
