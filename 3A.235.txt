"Peter Palmer…parish of St. Stephen…planter for and in consideration of the sum of one hundred punds sterling money to me in hand paid…by my Negroe woman named Phillis (the daughter of Amey formerly the property of Joseph Palmer, but now free) have manumised…set free the above named Negro Woman Phillis, her son Tomboy, her daughter Grace, her grand son William (the son of Grace) and a young woman named Beck."
