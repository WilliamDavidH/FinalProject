"Edgar Wells…for and in consideration of the fidelity and services o the following Negro Slave to wit Daniel alias Moses (Latelypurchased of Malcolm Ross) and also for divers other considerations me thereunto moving have manumitted, emancipated and set free…the above mentioned Negro slave in manner following, that is to say at and after the expiration of the term of ten years ensuing to commence from and immediately after the day and date of these presents."
