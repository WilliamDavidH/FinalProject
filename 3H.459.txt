"\"…I Edgar Wells, for and in consideration of the fidelity and services of the following negro slave, to wit Dido, and also for divers other considerations me hereunto moving, have manumitted, emancipated, and set free, and by these presents do manumit, emancipate, and set free, the above mentioned negro slave in manner following that is to say, immediately after the sealing and delivering these presents to have and to enjoy her freedom during the rest of her life, and I do hereby relinquish my right, title, claim and demand to the aforesaid negro woman Dido…"
