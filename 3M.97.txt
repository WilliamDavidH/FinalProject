" \"Retusah Cox…for and in consideration of the favor and affection which I have for and bear towards my Negro woman slave named Patience, for & on accord of her fidelity, honesty, & integrity & also for divers other good causes &  valuable considerations me hereunto moving and also for and in consideration of the sum of ten shillings sterling money to me in hand paid…have emancipated, manumised, released and from the bonds of slavery forever set free… the said negro woman slaved named Patience.\""
