"Know all men by these presents that I, Alexander James of the City of Charleston, Merchant, for and in consideration of the sum of three hundred and seventy dollars to me in hand paid at and before the sealing and delivery of  these presents by my Black Slave called Maria, and also for other good causes and considerations me hereunto moving have manumitted, enfranchised and from the bonds of slavery set free, and by these presents for myself, my  heirs, executors and administrators do hereby discharge my said Black Slave named Maria together with her future issue, to hold and enjoy such her and their manumission and freedom, free from any and all claims of service  whatsoever by me or any person or persons whomsoever from the day of the date hereof."
