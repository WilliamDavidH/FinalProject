"Charleston District, Deed of Manumission. William Hasell Gibbs of Charleston…in consieration of twenty five pounds paid me on behalf o the female child called Margaret the daughter of my muster wench called Margaret or Peggy and for divers good causes and considerations…have manumitted enfranchised set free and discharged the said child Margaret of and from all manner of bondage and slavery whatsoever, reserving however to myself alone a right of guardianship, over the said child so far only as may conduce to or be evidently calculated to promote its welfare and advantage and protect it from the ill designs of others and not otherwise for and during the space of seven years from the date hereof at which time is is hoped she will have sufficient discretion to conduct herself with prudence."
