"Know all men by these presents that I, Carolina, otherwise called Carolina Lamboll a freed Negro man for and consideration as well of the sums of twenty shilling currency to me in hand paid and of whichh I hereby acknowledge the receipt as of the the love and affection which I now have and bear to Jane my wife lately purchased by me of Ms. Rachel Caw have liberated, emancipated, manumised and set free by these presents do for my self, my executors and administrators liberate, emancipate, manumise and set free the said negro woman slave Jane together with her issue and increase. (19 January 1779)"
