"Joseph Manyarne, St. Johns Parish, Planter, for \"the favor & affection which I have for and bear towards my three slaves named Catey and her two children named Patty and Isaac, for and in consideration of their fidelity, honesty,  and integrity…and for consideration of ten shillings sterling money to me in hand…truly paid…I have enfranchised, manumised, released and from the bonds of slavery forever set free,."
