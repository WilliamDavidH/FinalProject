"John Calvert and John Gross both of Charlestown…for and in consideration of the sum of one hundred pounds…to us in hand paid…by Negroe man named Sam lately belonging to the estate of Benjamin Smith Esquire deceased and sold t us by Thomas Smith and Thomas L. Smith Esquire executors of the said Benjamin Smith Esquire. We the said….in consideration of the aforesaid sum…do manumit, give and grant freedom from slavery to the said Negro man Sam henceforth and forever."
