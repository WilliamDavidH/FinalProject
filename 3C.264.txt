"I, Cloudy Morand of the City of Charleston in the State of SOuth Carolina, a free mulatto man send greeting. Know ye that I the said Cloudy Morand for and in consideration of the sum of ten shillings sterling money to me in hand well and truly paid…have manumitted, enfranchised, set free and discharged…my mulatto or muster child named Cloudy from all and all manner of bondage and slavery whatsoever. "
