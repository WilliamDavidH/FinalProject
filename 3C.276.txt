"Silvia Stone, a free negro woman who died about fortune years ago had a Negro woman named Cloe which she alwas wished and intended should be free, after the payment of a debt due to William Hopton Esq. Dec'd, which said debt was to be paid by the said Cloe. This will certify whom it may concern that the said Cloe has fully paid the said debt and that she is free. Also her son named Moses a boy about five years old. (Signed Nath Russell)"
