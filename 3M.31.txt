"\"Hugh Wilson of Wadmelaw Island, planter, for and in consideration of the sum of fifty pounds to me in hand paid…by Patrick McDonald of Charleston…do bargain and sell and deliver…a mulatto boy named Charles [8 Feb. 1799]

 I the within named Patrick McDonald do hereby manumit & forever set free from the yoke of slavery the within named mulatto boy named Charles. 1 April 1799"
