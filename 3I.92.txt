"I, Benjamin Perry executor of the last will and testament of Abram Ladson of the Parish of St. Andrew in the District of Charleston in the state aforesaid in consideration of the sum of eighty eight pounds one shilling and nine pence 	sterling to me in hand paid before the delivery of these presents, have manumitted, released and discharged from slavery and by thesee presents do manumit, release and discharge from slavery a negro man named Mingo.
