"I, Sarah Mullins of Charleston in the state aforesaid do in consideration of the sum of five shillings sterling to me paid by a Mulatto man named Ned, belonging to Colonel Bernard Beekman and of the sum of money herein after 	mentioned to be paid to me during his life and for divers other causes and considerations do hereby manumit and from the bond of slavery forever set free my Mulatto boy named Ned of the age of fifteen years or thereabouts, son of 	Ned above named, to hold and anjou such manumission and freedom to said boy Ned forever he or his father, praying to me during the term of my natural life, provided the said boy Ned shall so long live the sum of five dollars every 	month."
